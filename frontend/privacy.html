Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is sacred to us. This policy explains how Peace Vision collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact form (name, email, phone, message)
  • Book a session or consultation
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Create an account on our platform
  • Make a payment for services

We may also collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited through standard web server logs and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support
  • Confirm and manage your session bookings
  • Send you the newsletter and healing content you've subscribed to
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure payment processing via Stripe (we never store card details)
  • Regular security reviews and updates

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website (e.g., email, hosting)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of others

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language and other settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser or our cookie consent banner.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@peacevision.com.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe — Payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)

  • Anthropic Claude AI — Our Soul Guide AI chat feature

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or by email.
